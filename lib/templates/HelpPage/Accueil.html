{t}Available features{/t}

{t}according to your permissions, click on corresponding tab{/t}:

  • {t}Admin{/t}: {t}configure Onlogistics (users, catalogues, alerts, etc...){/t},
  • {t}Back office{/t}: {t}define your logistics environment: chains, products purchased or sold, actors (customers, suppliers, service providers...) etc...{/t},
  • {t}Nomenclatures{/t}: {t}define and store product nomenclatures,{/t}
  • {t}Pricing{/t}: {t}define offer on sales, seasonalities and discounts by customer categories,{/t}
  • {t}Orders{/t}: {t}place customer or supplier orders,{/t}
  • {t}Orders follow-up{/t}: {t}view orders state, billings and payments{/t},
  • {t}Cash flow{/t}: {t}configure accounting data,{/t}
  • {t}Work order{/t}: {t}execute operations regroupings, build and print carriage orders{/t},
  • {t}Stock{/t}: {t}view stock quantities, stock state by location, movements and inventory history and expected movements{/t},
  • {t}Documents{/t}: {t}reprint existing documents{/t},
  • {t}Scoreboard{/t}: {t}turnover statistics and performance indicators{/t},
  • {t}Tasks{/t}: {t}validate tasks, view history and plannings{/t},
  • {t}Flight{/t}: {t}execute operations related to flights (only in aeronautical context).{/t}.

 

{t}Some useful tips for increasing your productivity{/t}

  • {t}Full screen display{/t}: {t}press the "F11" key{/t},
  • {t}Display help{/t}: {t}where available, use "Contextual help" button (on the screen up right corner){/t},
  • {t}Send a mail to technical support{/t}: {t}click on "Technical support" link (at the top of every screen){/t},
  • {t}Sign out{/t}: {t}click on the "Sign out" link (up right){/t},
  • {t}Search{/t}: {t}most of the screens have a search engine, fill in wished search criteria and click on "Search" button to launch the search.{/t}
