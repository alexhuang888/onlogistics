{t}Purpose{/t}

{t}View completed stock movements, in relation to an order (normal entries and issues) or not (dislocation, client refusal, damage etc...){/t}.

 

{t}Content{/t}

  • {t}The search engine{/t}: {t}use the search engine to find desired movements and click on "New search" to reset your search criteria{/t},
  • {t}The list of movements{/t}: {t}it contains the results of your search or, by default, only last 24 hours movements{/t}.

 

{t}Available actions{/t}

  • {t}print a delivery order for a movement if it was not yet printed{/t}: {t}select movement and click on "Delivery order". To reprint this document, go to "Documents" tab and "Documents reprinting" sub tab{/t},
  • {t}cancel an unexpected movement{/t} {t}stored by error or{/t} {t}record a return after a customer refusal{/t}: {t}select movement and click on "Delete"{/t},
  • {t}export displayed list to CSV (excel compatible format){/t}: {t}click on "Export" button{/t}.
