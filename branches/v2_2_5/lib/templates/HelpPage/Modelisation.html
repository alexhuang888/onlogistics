{t}Back office tab allows you to do the following operations:{/t}:

  • {t}define your chains for products, carriage, classes and services{/t},
  • {t}define the products and SN/Lots that you are selling, buying or using{/t},
  • {t}define links between chains and products{/t},
  • {t}configure actors (customers, suppliers, service providers...){/t},
  • {t}define delivery and supplying zones, that will be used for carriage operations regrouping{/t},
  • {t}define services and price lists in relation with classes bookings, carriage services bookings or maintenance services bookings{/t}.
