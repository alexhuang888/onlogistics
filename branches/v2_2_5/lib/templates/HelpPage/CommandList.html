{t}Purpose{/t}

{t}This screen allows you to view product orders follow-up, issue or view invoices and payments related to these orders and manage instalments.{/t}

 

{t}Content{/t}

  • {t}The search engine{/t}: {t}use the search engine to find desired orders and click on "New search" to reset your search criteria{/t},
  • {t}the list of product orders{/t} {t}matching your search criteria is displayed.{/t}

 

{t}Available actions{/t}

  • {t}View delivery details for an order{/t} {t}by clicking on its number in the list{/t},
  • {t}view comment provided during order{/t}: {t}place your cursor on the order number in the list{/t},

  • {t}unlock an order{/t}: {t}select locked order by clicking on the corresponding check box and click on the "Unlock" button{/t},
  • {t}issue an invoice{/t} : {t}select order to charge by clicking on the corresponding check box and click on the "Charge" button{/t},
  • {t}record an instalment{/t}: {t}select order by clicking on the corresponding check box and click on the "Instalment" button{/t},
  • {t}export displayed list to CSV (excel compatible format){/t}: {t}click on "Export" button{/t}.
